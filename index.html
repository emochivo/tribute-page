G-DRAGON

The Greatest of All Time

G-Dragon (real name: Kwon Ji-yong) is a rapper, songwriter, composer, and the leader of K-pop legendary boy group BIGBANG. G-Dragon's stage name was based on his real name: G (Ji) and Dragon, which is "Yong" in Korean.

G-Dragon has been working in the entertainment industry since little, when he was a member of a tribute group to a Korean band named Roo'ra.

He participated in a dance competition and caught the attention from Lee Soo-man, the founder of SM Entertainment. G-Dragon was a trainer in SM for 5 years, before being persuaded by Yang Hyun-suk, the founder of YG Entertainment and also the former member of Seo Taiji and Boys.

After doing some other training for another 6 years, our GD finally debuted in a 5-member hip hop boy group, BIGBANG!!
